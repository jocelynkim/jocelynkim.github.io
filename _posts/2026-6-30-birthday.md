Sorry these are crappy distorted gifs I'm too lazy to figure out how to export these nicely. Also we were kind of going for shitty graphics anyway so it's fine.........

It was a great project for me to learn Godot for the first time! I was really surprised at how lightweight the engine is. I feel like Rock Lee taking off his weights when I go from working in Unity to working in Godot.
I had a pretty good rhythm going on, where I would spend an hour almost every morning working on the game. 

Ultimately I ran out of time to wrap up the project and fix outstanding bugs, but I'm still really happy with how much I learned! If I were to return to this project I'd like to think a bit harder about the bullet hell implementation. It's pretty hard to find the right balance of fun in a bullet hell-like game.

Also... I want to work with my friends more!!! If there are any rich billionaire oil princes out there that want to fund my woke indie games, please hit me up.

And with that... goodnight and happy Pride month! 🌈
